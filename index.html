About Me

Hello! I’m Mohamed Ashif M, a pre-final year B.Tech student in Artificial Intelligence and Data Science at Stella Mary’s College of Engineering. I’m deeply passionate about building intuitive, data-driven, and visually engaging web experiences that create real impact.

My strength lies at the intersection of data and design — I love transforming ideas into interactive, user-centric frontends using React, JavaScript, and Tailwind CSS, while also understanding the logic and structure behind the scenes with MySQL and PostgreSQL. Whether it’s crafting smooth user interfaces or managing efficient databases, I aim to bridge creativity with functionality in every project I work on.

Beyond coding, I’m an active leader in the “Byte Bash Blitz” tech community, where I collaborate with peers, mentor juniors, and contribute to creating a vibrant learning environment. These experiences have strengthened my teamwork, communication, and problem-solving skills — all while fueling my curiosity to explore new technologies.

I’m a curious learner, creative thinker, and results-driven developer who believes in continuous growth through experimentation and collaboration. I’m eager to apply my skills in AI, web development, and data systems to build meaningful, scalable, and innovative solutions as part of a forward-thinking team.
